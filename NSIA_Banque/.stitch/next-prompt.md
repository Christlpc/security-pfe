A high-density financial dashboard for NSIA Banque.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Atmosphere**: Financial Enterprise Grade, Prestigious, Dense layout with compact elements.
- **Color Palette**:
  - Deep Navy Blue (`#0B192C`): Primary branding, header fills, primary buttons.
  - Navy Steel (`#1E3E62`): Secondary gradients, interactive borders.
  - Amber Gold (`#D97706`): Accent highlights, pending flags.
  - Emerald Mint (`#10B981`): Success KPIs, conversion charts.
- **Borders & Corners**: Clean thin lines, roundness of 8px (`rounded-lg`), very soft shadows.
- **Whitespace Strategy**: Dense layout, replacement of pure white space with pearl white containers (`#F8FAFC`).

**Page Structure:**
1. Global Navigation & Bank Header (incorporating Navy theme, quick user switcher, active branch/bank info).
2. KPI Ribbon: 4 high-density statistics cards (Total Sales, Conversion Rate with trend chart, Pending Approvals, Commission Earned).
3. Main Grid:
   - Left (2/3): Interactive Simulation Trends (Recharts style line chart) and a dense Recent Simulations Table with micro-badges.
   - Right (1/3): Product distribution radial chart and a timeline of recent broker/gestionnaire actions.
